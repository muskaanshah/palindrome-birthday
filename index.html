Check out if your Birthdate is Palidrome.

Enter your birthdate and we will tell you if your birthdate is a palindrome or not.

This app checks your birthdate in 6 formats yyyy-mm-dd, dd-mm-yyyy, mm-dd-yy, mm-dd-yy, dd-mm-yy, yy-mm-dd

Eg: if your birthdate is 01 Aug 1995, then app will check for 19950801, 01081995, 080195, 1081995
